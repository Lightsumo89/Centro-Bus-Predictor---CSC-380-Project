Help & Support

Welcome to the Centro Help Page. Below are helpful instructions and support resources for using our public transportation tools.

Quick Start Guide

Use the Map to see when buses are arriving at specific stops.

Click on any bus stop marker to view prediction times.

Use the MENU above to visit other pages like FAQs and route listings.

Using the Map

Our interactive map helps you view bus arrival predictions in real time:

    Click on any stop icon to see predicted bus arrival times.

    The map updates approximately every 15 seconds with new data.

    Enable location services on your device to automatically find nearby stops.

Troubleshooting

    Map not loading? Ensure your browser supports JavaScript and that you're connected to the internet.

    Predictions not updating? Refresh the page; predictions are refreshed every 15 seconds automatically.

    Can't find your stop? Zoom in on the map or use location services to highlight nearby stops.
